Your questions, answered

We know you have questions before trusting a new platform with your income. Here are the honest answers.

Your US account is held at Lead Bank through Stripe's Bridge — real US banking infrastructure subject to US banking regulations. This isn't an experimental setup.

Cenoa itself operates on a non-custodial model — meaning you control your wallet. Your funds aren't sitting in Cenoa's accounts.

Monthly limit: $150,000 for receiving payments. Single payment limit: $4,000 from accounts not in your name (like marketplaces). Amounts over this are automatically returned.

For most freelancers and remote workers, these limits are more than enough. If you need higher limits, business accounts are available.

Individual accounts: about 3 minutes with just your ID. Business accounts take 3-7 days as they require additional verification through Bridge.

This is honest setup time — faster than banks or Payoneer, but not completely instant. Plan accordingly for your first payment.
